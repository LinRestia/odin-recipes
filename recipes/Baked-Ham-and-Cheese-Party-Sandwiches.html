Baked Ham and Cheese Party Sandwiches

Description

Great tasting sandwiches that can fit in your hand. They make for great finger food.

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup melted butter
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons Dijon mustard
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons poppy seeds
  • 1 tablespoon dried minced onion
  • 24 mini sandwich rolls
  • 1 pound thinly sliced cooked deli ham
  • 1 pound thinly sliced Swiss cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F(175 degrees C). Grease 9x13 inch baking dish.
  2. Mix butter, Dijon mustard, Worcestershire sauce, poppy seeds, and dried onions together in a bowl.
  3. Seperate top from bottom of the mini rolls. Then place bottom piece into the prepared baking dish.
  4. Layer about half the ham onto the rolls. Arrange the Swiss cheese over the ham and top with remaining ham slices in a layer
  5. Place the tops of the rolls onto the sandwiches. Pour the mustard mixture evenly over the rolls.
  6. Bake in the preheated oven until the rolls are lightly brown and the cheese has melted, about 20 minutes.
  7. Slice into individual rolls through the ham and cheese layers to serve.
